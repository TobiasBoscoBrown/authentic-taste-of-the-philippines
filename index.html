The people behind the counter

Meet Auntie Juliet

Authentic Taste of the Philippines is run by Auntie Juliet and the ladies who cook, plate, and serve every order themselves. Reviewers from the Filipino community write that eating here feels like visiting family, the kind of welcome and cooking you find in a tita's kitchen back home.

The menu is short on purpose and long on heart: a handful of everyday plates, a board of fried snacks priced by the piece, and a rotating set of specials cooked fresh that day. If you grew up on this food, it will take you back. If you did not, this is the warmest possible introduction.

Good to know

Where is Authentic Taste of the Philippines located?

We are at 433 Caldwell Blvd in Nampa, Idaho, right next to the Asian grocery store. Look for us along Caldwell Boulevard.

What are your hours?

We are open Tuesday through Saturday from 11 AM to 6 PM, and closed Sunday and Monday. Holiday hours may vary, so call ahead on holidays.

Do you take orders ahead or do pickup?

Yes. We offer dine in and curbside pickup. Call us at (208) 250-7630 to order ahead, especially for lumpia and specials.

What is the most popular dish?

Regulars love the lechon kawali rice meal and the lumpia, and the ube shake is the one everyone photographs. Many of our home cooked specials rotate daily.

Is the food authentic Filipino cooking?

Yes. The kitchen is family run and reviewers from the Filipino community call it the real thing, lutong bahay made the way it is back home.

Hungry for home?

Call ahead for lumpia and specials, or come see us on Caldwell Boulevard. Tuesday through Saturday, 11 to 6.
