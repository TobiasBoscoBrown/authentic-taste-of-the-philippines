Rice Meals and Lechon

Crispy fried pork belly and rich roast classics served family style with steamed rice.

Good for

First timers, big appetites, and anyone who misses real lutong bahay pork.

How long

Made fresh to order, usually ready in a short wait while you watch it come together.

What it is

Our rice meals start with crispy lechon kawali, pork belly simmered until tender then deep fried so the skin shatters and the meat stays juicy. Plated with a generous scoop of steamed white rice, it is the plate regulars come back for week after week.

On the menu

Crispy Pata (special)$30

Crispy pata is our show stopper special, a whole deep fried pork hock with crackling skin. Ask if it is on that day.
